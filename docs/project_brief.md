# Project Brief: Selasar Digital Website

## 1. Executive Summary
**Project Name:** Selasar Digital Corporate Website
**Client/Brand:** Selasar Digital
**Project Type:** Software House Portfolio and Service Website

**Objective:** To design and develop a modern, premium, and highly responsive website for **Selasar Digital**, a professional software house. The website will serve as the primary digital touchpoint to showcase the company's services, portfolio, and technical expertise, ultimately aiming to attract new clients and generate leads.

## 2. Company Overview
**Selasar Digital** is a forward-thinking software house specializing in crafting high-quality digital solutions. Our core expertise spans across web development, mobile application development, and UI/UX design. We focus on building scalable, efficient, and user-centric digital products tailored to business needs.

## 3. Project Objectives
*   **Establish Brand Identity:** Visually communicate professionalism, innovation, and technical excellence.
*   **Showcase Expertise:** Highlight core services and technical stack clearly.
*   **Showcase Portfolio:** Present past projects (Web, Mobile, UI/UX) with detailed case studies.
*   **Lead Generation:** Provide seamless ways for prospective clients to contact the team or request a quote (e.g., WhatsApp integration, contact forms).
*   **Performance & SEO:** Ensure fast load times, mobile responsiveness, and strong foundational SEO to rank well on search engines.

## 4. Target Audience
*   **B2B Clients (Startups, SMEs, Enterprises):** Businesses looking for reliable technology partners to build or scale their digital products.
*   **Founders & Entrepreneurs:** Individuals seeking development teams to bring their app or platform ideas to life.
*   **IT Managers & Directors:** Tech decision-makers looking to outsource specific technical modules.

## 5. Core Services to Highlight
*   **Web Application Development:** Custom web apps, landing pages, enterprise software.
*   **Mobile App Development:** Cross-platform native and hybrid mobile applications.
*   **UI/UX Design:** User-centered design, prototyping, and wireframing.

## 6. Website Structure (Sitemap)
1.  **Home Page:** High-impact hero section, brief "About Us," summary of services, featured portfolio carousel, and a call-to-action (CTA).
2.  **About Us:** Company mission, vision, team overview, and core values.
3.  **Services:** Detailed breakdown of each service offering with technologies used.
4.  **Portfolio / Work:** Filterable gallery of past projects (Categories: All, Web, Mobile, UI/UX).
5.  **Testimonials:** Quotes, feedback from past clients, and brand credibility markers.
6.  **Blog / Insights (Optional):** Articles on tech trends, company updates, and case studies.
7.  **Contact Us:** Inquiry form, location, email, and direct WhatsApp links.

## 7. Design & Branding Guidelines
*   **Aesthetic:** Modern, clean, and premium. Utilization of sophisticated UI elements (glassmorphism, subtle micro-animations, smooth scrolling).
*   **Color Palette:** Use a curated technology-focused color palette (e.g., professional slates/charcoals alongside vibrant accents for CTAs to suggest both reliability and innovation).
*   **Typography:** Modern sans-serif Google Fonts (e.g., Inter, Outfit, or Roboto) for high readability and a sleek look.

| Layer | Technology |
|---|---|
| **Backend** | Laravel 12 |
| **Templating** | Laravel Blade (SSR) |
| **JavaScript** | Alpine.js (lightweight interactivity) |
| **Styling** | Tailwind CSS v4 |
| **Build Tool** | Vite |
| **Database** | MySQL |
| **Real-time** | Laravel Reverb |
| **Queue** | Laravel Horizon + Redis |
| **Theme** | Calm, Elegant & Professional |

*   **Content Management System (CMS) / Dashboard:** A custom Laravel 12 dashboard to manage Portfolio Projects, Testimonials, and other dynamic content.
*   **Analytics:** Google Analytics & Search Console integration to track growth and conversions.

## 9. Project Phases & Timeline
*   **Phase 1: Discovery & Planning**
    *   Requirement gathering, competitor analysis, and finalized sitemap.
*   **Phase 2: UI/UX Design**
    *   Wireframing, high-fidelity mockups, and interactive prototypes.
*   **Phase 3: Frontend & Backend Development**
    *   Building the UI components, integrating animations, and connecting the data layer.
*   **Phase 4: Content Integration & Portfolio Setup**
    *   Adding project descriptions, images, and text content.
*   **Phase 5: Testing, QA & SEO Optimization**
    *   Cross-browser testing, mobile responsiveness checks, performance tuning, and on-page SEO.
*   **Phase 6: Deployment & Launch**
    *   Final production build, go-live, and handover.

## 10. Success Metrics
*   Increased website traffic and lower bounce rate.
*   High conversion rate on the "Contact Us" or "Hire Us" forms.
*   Fast page load speeds (Google Lighthouse score > 90).
*   Positive user feedback on design, navigation, and portfolio visibility.
